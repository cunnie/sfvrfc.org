San Francisco Vintage Rugby Football Club

San Francisco Vintage Rugby Football Club is a group of unaffiliated rugby people who gather together to play touch rugby Sunday mornings 9am - 11am. Players of any skill, gender, age, or orientation are welcome — no experience necessary.

We meet at Saint Mary's Playground (map). The pitch is grass, not astroturf, so bring cleats. Also, bring a red shirt and a blue shirt: we divide into teams, Blues versus Reds.

Text us here before you show up — we sometimes change location (field closures).

Do you live in the East Bay? You may find our sister group, the East Bay Vintage Touch Rugby (EBVTR), more convenient. Find more about them here.
